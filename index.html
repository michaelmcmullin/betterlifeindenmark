Denmark's Standard of Living

How Denmark compares to other OECD Countries

The World Economic Forum tweeted a graph showing that Denmark has the best work-life balance out of all the OECD countries. The original data came from the OECD Better Life Initiative, 2016.

This data has been reproduced below as a bar chart. Each value was scaled to a range between 1 and 100, representing the lowest and highest figure in each category respectively. From there, the OECD average was calculated, and each bar represents how far above or below the average Denmark scores in each category.
